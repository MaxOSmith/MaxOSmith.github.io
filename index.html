Max O. Smith

I am a third year Ph.D. student at the University of Michigan working with Michael P. Wellman. Previously I was an intern with Aaron Courville at the Montreal Institute for Learning Algorithms. I am interested in continual learning, meta-learning, deep learning, and education.

As an undergraduate I had the absolute pleasure to work with many brilliant advisors: Honglak Lee, Rada Mihalcea, and Andrew DeOrio. I also spent my summers at Google and Sandia National Labs.
